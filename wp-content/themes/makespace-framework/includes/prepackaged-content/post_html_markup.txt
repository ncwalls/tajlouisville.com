<h2>HTML Tags</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>These supported tags come from the WordPress.com code <a title="Code" href="http://en.support.wordpress.com/code/">FAQ</a>.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Address Tag</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:html -->
<address>1 Infinite Loop<br>Cupertino, CA 95014<br>United States</address>
<!-- /wp:html -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Anchor Tag (aka. Link)</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This is an example of a <a title="Apple" href="http://apple.com">link</a>.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Abbreviation Tag</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The abbreviation <abbr title="Seriously">srsly</abbr> stands for "seriously".</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Acronym Tag (<em>deprecated in HTML5</em>)</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The acronym <acronym title="For The Win">ftw</acronym> stands for "for the win".</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Big Tag <strong>(<em>deprecated in HTML5</em>)</strong></strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>These tests are a <big>big</big> deal, but this tag is no longer supported in HTML5.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Cite Tag</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>"Code is poetry." --<cite>Automattic</cite></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Code Tag</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>You will learn later on in these tests that <code>word-wrap: break-word;</code> will be your best friend.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Delete Tag</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This tag will let you <del>strikeout text</del>, but this tag is no longer supported in HTML5 (use the <code>&lt;strike&gt;</code> instead).</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Emphasize Tag</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The emphasize tag should <em>italicize</em> text.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Insert Tag</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This tag should denote <ins>inserted</ins> text.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Keyboard Tag</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This scarcely known tag emulates <kbd>keyboard text</kbd>, which is usually styled like the <code>&lt;code&gt;</code> tag.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Preformatted Tag</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This tag styles large blocks of code.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:preformatted -->
<pre class="wp-block-preformatted">.post-title {
	margin: 0 0 5px;
	font-weight: bold;
	font-size: 38px;
	line-height: 1.2;
	and here's a line of some really, really, really, really long text, just to see how the PRE tag handles it and to find out how it overflows;
}</pre>
<!-- /wp:preformatted -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Quote Tag</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><q>Developers, developers, developers...</q> --Steve Ballmer</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Strike Tag <strong>(<em>deprecated in HTML5</em>)</strong></strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This tag shows <span style="text-decoration: line-through;">strike-through text</span></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Strong Tag</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This tag shows <strong>bold<strong> text.</strong></strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Subscript Tag</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Getting our science styling on with H<sub>2</sub>O, which should push the "2" down.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Superscript Tag</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Still sticking with science and Isaac Newton's E = MC<sup>2</sup>, which should lift the 2 up.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Teletype Tag <strong>(<em>deprecated in HTML5</em>)</strong></strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This rarely used tag emulates <tt>teletype text</tt>, which is usually styled like the <code>&lt;code&gt;</code> tag.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Variable Tag</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This allows you to denote <var>variables</var>.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
